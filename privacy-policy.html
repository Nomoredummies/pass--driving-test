Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

This page outlines how the Pass Driving Test app handles privacy information in both English and Simplified Chinese.

Privacy Policy

  1. Information We Collect

    To provide account, route display, and navigation features, we may collect:

    • Account information: email address, optional phone number, nickname.
    • Device and usage information: device model, operating system version, application logs for troubleshooting and quality improvement.
    • Location data: collected only when authorized, used to display nearby test centres and for navigation.

  2. Purpose of Use

    We use the collected information to:

    • Provide and maintain product functions such as login/unlock, route display, and navigation.
    • Deliver customer service and user support.
    • Enhance security and prevent abuse (for example, fraud prevention).
    • Conduct statistics and product improvement using aggregated or de-identified data only.

  3. Data Storage and Security

    Data may be stored in third-party cloud services (for example Firebase by Google) or on your local device.

    We take reasonable measures such as access control and encryption to safeguard data.

    We do not store your real-time location data long-term on our servers.

  4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

    We do not sell your personal information. Disclosure only occurs when:

    • Required by law or by government authorities.
    • Necessary to protect life, property, or rights of you, us, or the public (for example anti-fraud).
    • Needed for mergers, restructuring, or asset/business transfers, with legal compliance and prior notice.

  5. Third-Party Services

    The app integrates or may redirect to third-party services:

    • Google Maps or Apple Maps for maps and navigation.
    • Firebase (by Google) for account services, authentication, cloud storage, and analytics.

    These service providers may collect or process necessary information in line with their own privacy policies.

  6. Third-Party Links

    When you visit third-party websites or applications, your information may be subject to their privacy policies.

    Please review those policies before submitting any data.

  7. Your Rights

    You may:

    • Withdraw location permission at any time in system settings.
    • Delete your account in-app or request deletion via email.
    • Request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data except where retention is required by law.

  8. Before Contacting Us

    If you have questions, feedback, or complaints about this policy, please contact us via the email below.

    We will respond after verifying your request.

  9. Coach Information and Third-Party Responsibility

    1. The app may display materials or contact information from third parties such as independent coaches. This information is provided by them or sourced publicly and is not collected as your personal information.
    2. If learners share personal information with third parties (for example coaches), they must confirm the third party’s privacy policy directly.
    3. We do not store, process, or use communications, payment details, or other sensitive data between learners and coaches.
    4. Any disputes or privacy issues arising from engagement with third-party coaches are solely between the user and the third party. We assume no responsibility.
    5. Routes and tips provided in this app are for reference only. Always follow the official guidelines and requirements issued by NZTA.

  10. In-App Purchases

    The app offers optional paid features or content through Apple’s In-App Purchase mechanism.

    • All payments are processed by Apple. We do not collect or store your payment card or billing information.
    • Purchase confirmation, billing, and refund policies follow Apple’s App Store terms.
    • Purchased features or content are linked to your account and cannot be transferred or refunded unless required by law or by Apple policy.
    • For parental controls, please manage in-app purchases through Apple Family Settings.

  11. Navigation and Usage Data

    We use in-app navigation to provide a smoother experience. Anonymous usage data such as page views and button taps may be collected to analyse and improve usability. This statistical data cannot identify individuals.

  12. Contact Us

    For any questions or concerns, please contact us at passdrivingtestnow@gmail.com.

  13. Effective Date

    Effective date: valid from the app release date. Major changes to this policy will be announced within the app.

隐私政策

  1. 我们收集的信息

    为了提供账号、路线展示与导航等功能，我们可能收集：

    • 账户信息：邮箱、可选手机号、昵称。
    • 设备与使用信息：设备型号、系统版本、应用使用日志（用于故障定位与质量改进）。
    • 位置数据：仅在您授权时用于显示附近考点及发起导航。

  2. 信息的使用目的

    我们将上述信息用于：

    • 提供与维护产品功能（账号登录/解锁、路线与导航）。
    • 客服与用户支持。
    • 安全防护与防滥用（例如防刷、防作弊）。
    • 统计与产品改进（仅使用汇总或去标识化数据）。

  3. 信息存储与安全

    数据可能存储在第三方云服务（如 Google 提供的 Firebase）或保存在您的本地设备。

    我们采取合理的安全措施（访问控制、传输加密等）保护数据安全。

    我们不会在自有服务器上长期保存您的实时定位数据。

  4. 信息共享与披露

    我们不会出售您的个人信息，仅在以下必要情形下披露：

    • 遵守法律法规或行政、司法要求。
    • 保护您、我们或公众的生命财产安全与合法权益（例如反欺诈、风控）。
    • 发生合并、分立、重组或资产、业务转让时，在符合法律法规且履行告知义务的前提下进行。

  5. 第三方服务

    本应用集成或可能跳转以下第三方服务：

    • Google 地图或 Apple 地图：用于地图展示与导航。
    • Firebase（Google 提供）：用于账号、认证、云存储或分析。

    上述第三方可能根据其隐私政策收集和处理必要信息。

  6. 第三方链接

    当您跳转至第三方网站或应用时，您的信息可能受该第三方隐私政策约束。

    请在提交任何信息前查阅并理解其政策。

  7. 您的权利

    您可以：

    • 在系统设置中随时撤回定位权限。
    • 在应用内删除账号或通过邮箱联系我们请求删除数据。
    • 请求访问、更正或删除您的个人信息（法律另有规定的除外）。

  8. 联系我们前的提示

    如对本政策有疑问、意见或投诉，欢迎联系下方邮箱。

    我们将在核验后尽快处理。

    1. 本应用可能展示第三方（如独立教练）提供的资料与联系方式，该类信息由第三方自行提供或公开渠道获取，不属于本应用收集的用户隐私信息。
    2. 学员如需向第三方提供个人信息，应直接与第三方确认其隐私政策，本应用对此不予干预。
    3. 本应用不存储、不处理、不使用学员与教练之间的通信内容、支付记录或其他敏感数据。
    4. 因用户选择与第三方教练发生的任何信息共享或纠纷，均由用户与相关第三方自行解决，本应用不承担责任。
    5. 本应用提供的路线和提示仅供参考，请务必遵循 NZTA 官方指南和要求。

    • 付款、账单及退款政策遵循 Apple App Store 的条款。
    • 已购买的功能或内容将与您的账户绑定，不可转让或退款（法律或 Apple 政策另有规定的除外）。
    • 如为未成年人使用，请通过 Apple 家庭共享或家长控制管理应用内购买。

    本应用采用应用内导航方式提供服务。为优化用户体验，我们可能收集匿名的使用数据（如页面浏览、按钮点击等），仅用于统计分析与产品改进，不涉及个人身份识别。
